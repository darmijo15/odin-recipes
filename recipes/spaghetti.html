Spaghetti

Description

The spaghetti recipe is extremely simple. The sauce is a simple mix of seasoned ground beef and a jar of spaghetti sauce that is set to a low simmer for 30-60 minutes. Once the sauce is close to being done, cook the noodles and voila, you are ready to serve!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb spaghetti noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 20-24 oz jar of spaghetti sauce of choice
  • salt
  • pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • canola, vegetable, or olive oil
  • fresh shredded basil

Steps

  1. Place the ground beef on a cutting board and season with salt, pepper, garlic powder, and onion powder.
  2. Set a large pan to medium-high heat. After allowing the pan to come up to temperature, add 2 tbsps of oil to the pan.
  3. Add the seasoned ground beef to the pan. After letting it sear for 2 minutes, mix the beef until it is cooked to a light brown.
  4. Add the jar of spaghetti sauce to the pan. Mix it around with the beef.
  5. Cover the pan with a lid and set to a low-simmer. Allow the sauce to simmer for 30 minutes to an hour.
  6. After allowing the sauce to simmer for 20-40 minutes, it is time to begin the noodles.
  7. Fill a large pot a little more than halfway with water. Set the pot to high-heat and add a large pinch of salt to the water.
  8. Once the water begins to boil, add the noodles. Stir the noodles a bit so they loosen up and are submerged by the water.
  9. Turn the heat down to medium and allow to simmer for 10 minutes.
  10. Once 10 minutes has passed, use a pair of tongs or a slotted spoon the place the noodles in a strainer.
  11. Add about a 1/4 of a cup of the leftover pasta water and the noodles to the sauce and mix thoroughly.
  12. You are ready to serve, enjoy!
