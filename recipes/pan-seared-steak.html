Pan-seared Steak

Description

A perfectly seared steak is a lovely treat that can be enjoyed any week of the night. What's even better is that cooking it just right is not as hard as you may think. This recipe will give you step-by-step instructions for perfecting a garlic-butter basted steak cooked to your liking.

Ingredients

  • 8-16oz New York Strip or Ribeye
  • canola oil
  • 2 tablespoons of unsalted butter
  • 2 cloves of peeled garlic
  • fresh thyme
  • kosher salt
  • freshly cracked black pepper
